A chat-bot to modernize the way you plan group outings. Simpler logistics, hyper-personalization, and much more!

The Vibe Behind VibeBasket!

Friends

It's not uncommon for friends to have different ideas, making the planning process challenging and time-consuming. Whether you're looking for exciting dining experiences, fun-filled activities, or memorable destinations, VibeBasket has got you covered.

Budget

Each person in the group may have different financial limitations, making it difficult to find activities or venues that fit everyone's budget. However, VibeBasket is designed to tackle this issue head-on.

Time

Planning outings with friends becomes a hassle when everyone has different time constraints. With VibeBasket, you can say goodbye to the stress of time conflicts and confidently plan outings that suit everyone's availability.

It is not what you need, it is what you deserve!
